ADAMS-SIMULINK co-simulation of semi-active suspension of a 4-wheeled vehicle

  • Executed co-simulation between ADAMS and SIMULINK to analyze and control the damping force of an MR damper, leveraging the strengths of both platforms for enhanced simulation fidelity.

Graduation Thesis

NIT Rourkela

Design of chassis and suspension of bike ambulance

  • Utilized SOLIDWORKS to design the side-cart chassis and suspension system of bike ambulance.
  • Performed destructive test simulations using ANSYS Workbench, including crash analysis and torsional rigidity analysis, to ensure the structural integrity and safety of the design.
